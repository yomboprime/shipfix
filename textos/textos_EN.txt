=== Inicio

You are Gregory Donovan, an experienced space explorer known
across many systems. You were about to land on a planetoid, when
the computer has detected an imminent collision with a microme-
teor and in nanoseconds has decided your ejection, which happe-
ned 0.4 seconds before the impact.

You have landed safely with your emergency capsule, but it has
been damaged. Your ship has crash landed at the other side of
the planetoid and has a breakdown not diagnosticable by teleme-
try. You fear the worst: Hull integrity damage, pressure loss,
and insufficient air until you get a bailout, which can take
days.

You've got your space suit with you, equipped with propellers
and enough combustible.

Your mission now is get to the ship and see if you can repair
the breakdown. When you get near it, press 'fire' to get in.

=== LlegarNave

Congratulations for getting to the ship! Immediately you see
the breakdown, which fortunately is not serious but prevents the
takeoff. That's why the ship didn't get to you. The most impor-
tant, the living space and the air supply are still intact and
sealed, so your air supply has increased from a few hours to
weeks.

De momento estas a salvo, pero el mensaje de auxilio que se en-
vio en el accidente podria atraer tanto a amigos como enemigos.
Lo mejor es poner los pies en polvorosa.

Para reparar la nave deberas encontrar los siguientes objetos,
que han quedado desperdigados por la superficie del planetoide
durante el aterrizaje.

      Pulsa una tecla para ver el equipo que necesitas...
